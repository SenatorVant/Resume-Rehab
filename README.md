# 🚀 Resume Rehab

**Resume Rehab** is an AI-powered web application that helps users analyze, optimize, and rebuild their resumes into strong, job-ready documents. It focuses on improving clarity, impact, and ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatibility—so your resume actually gets seen.

🌐 **Live Site:** https://content-aggregator--vjain6.replit.app

---

## 💡 Overview

Resume Rehab acts like a personal resume coach. Users can input their resume and a job description, and the platform provides intelligent feedback, helping bridge the gap between their current resume and what employers are actually looking for.

Modern hiring systems rely heavily on automated screening (ATS), making optimization critical. Resume Rehab simplifies this process with AI-driven insights and actionable improvements.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Resume Analysis**
  - Evaluates structure, clarity, and effectiveness
  - Highlights weak or vague bullet points

- 🎯 **Job-Specific Optimization**
  - Compares resumes to job descriptions
  - Identifies missing skills and keywords

- 🤖 **AI-Powered Suggestions**
  - Rewrites bullet points for stronger impact
  - Improves wording and professionalism

- 📊 **ATS Compatibility Scoring**
  - Simulates how resumes perform in automated systems
  - Helps increase visibility to recruiters

- ⚡ **Instant Feedback**
  - Real-time results with actionable improvements
